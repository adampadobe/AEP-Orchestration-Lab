Scenario (orchestration) — expand for hotel.* event map

Default journey: Manchester city break → hotel.search on the home mock → this page fires hotel.availability.view, then hotel.filter.apply when guests toggle amenities, hotel.content.view when they open breakfast details, then room select → payment → hotel.checkout.abandon. Logo or “Book your stay” in the purple header sends hotel.funnel.exit (see hotelJourneyStage + hotelExitVia) before returning to the lab home — distinct from payment abandon. Abandon recovery can use hotelDemandForecastScore, hotelInventoryAvailable, and hotelResumeUrl as before. Booker vs stayer (lab): choosing Select on a rate opens a short dialog — self vs someone else. If someone else, capture first name, last name, and email (stored under hotelStayer* on hotel.room.select). On mock payment complete, hotel.booking.complete includes hotelStayerSameAsBooker and optional stayer fields; when the stayer differs, a second hotel.booking.stayerIdentified event carries the same itineraryId plus stayer identity for orchestration (linked, not merged — shell email/ECID remain the booker).

Premier Inn Breakfast

Lab content — mimics the on-site breakfast merchandising step before checkout.

Most Premier Inn hotels serve an unlimited cooked and continental breakfast. In a real journey this view often follows search refinement (e.g. parking) and precedes rate selection or basket.

  • Unlimited cooked & continental options (hotel-specific)
  • Kids eat free with paying adults (typical offer — verify live rules)
  • Great attach for email / push recovery after payment abandon
