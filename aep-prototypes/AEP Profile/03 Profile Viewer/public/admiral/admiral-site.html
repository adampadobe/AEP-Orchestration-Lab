Frequently asked
How is my Admiral score calculated? expand_more
Your score combines telematics signals (speed, braking, journey patterns), claim history, and the breadth of cover you hold across home, family, mobility and finances. The more we know, the more accurate it becomes.
When can I claim a reward? expand_more
Rewards unlock as your score reaches each milestone. Once unlocked, hit Claim from the Rewards page — most are sent to your email or app within minutes.
How do I add a partner or named driver? expand_more
Head to Profile → Household, or open Live chat and ask to add someone. We'll re-quote and let you know if anything changes.
Can I cancel anytime? expand_more
Yes — there's a 14-day cooling-off period from the start of your policy, and you can cancel any time after that. We'll just refund the unused portion.
