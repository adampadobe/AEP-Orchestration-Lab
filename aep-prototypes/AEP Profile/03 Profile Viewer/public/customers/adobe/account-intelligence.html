Account Intelligence — Adobe

Ingests (public): customer website, annual report / investor-deck hub (IR page), press releases, public job postings, recent news (RSS). Produces: one-page executive briefing, likely KPIs and pain points, AI and data maturity guess, and five tailored discovery questions. The server also collects image URLs from each page (meta tags, icons, hero/logo hints) and stores JPEG thumbnails under /customers/asset-cache/ (SVGs skipped; some CDNs may 404). Optional CRM notes add private context. PDF annual reports are not parsed here; use the IR hub page and discovery to go deeper.

CRM / meeting notes (optional)

Paste bullets from your CRM or last call. This is included in the synthesis step as retrieval context only; it is sent only to this app’s server (not to Adobe).
