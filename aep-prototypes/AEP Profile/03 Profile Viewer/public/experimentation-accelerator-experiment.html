Opportunity details

A/B Homepage Hero Image Test Active May 1, 2025 – Today
Administered by Adobe Journey Optimizer

Add an emotional appeal to increase revenue

Emotional messaging drives deeper engagement, leading to increased revenue. It taps into aspirational motivations like self-actualization, creativity, and growth. Studies show that emotionally charged messages are more memorable and influence decision-making more than solely rational ones.

If we emphasize emotional transformation rather than functional utility, users will feel a stronger connection and motivation to engage or convert, leading to increased revenue.

New text treatment examples

Try invoking emotions to make the offer more enticing. Instead of “Accomplish more with Acrobat Pro”, explore examples like:

  • Unlock a world of creative potential
  • Unleash your full potential
  • Bring your imagination to life
  • Supercharge your creativity

Rationale

Experiments analysis shows that making emotional appeals tend to lead to favorable outcomes. Previous experiments show higher click-through rates and increased revenue on landing pages for emotionally framed advertising than ones that lead with benefits vs. features.
