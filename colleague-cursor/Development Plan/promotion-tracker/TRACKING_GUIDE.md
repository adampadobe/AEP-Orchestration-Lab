# Tracking Guide – What and How to Track

Quick reference for what to log in each activity type and how it supports your Practice Lead case.

---

## 1. Mentoring & Technical Leadership

**Tool:** `python add_mentoring.py`

| What to track | Why it matters for Practice Lead |
|---------------|-----------------------------------|
| Who (name, role) | Unique mentees; sustained relationships (2+ sessions). |
| Topics | Evidence of capability building and technical leadership. |
| Duration | Total hours invested (dashboard shows mentoring hours). |
| Purpose | Links to “lead enablement,” “dedicated mentor to 1–2,” “grow presentation skills.” |
| Follow-up planned/completed | Shows sustained commitment. |

**Practice Lead bar:** Dedicated mentor to 1–2 team members; mentors 1–2 on presentation skills; leads enablement sessions.

## 2. Product / Engineering Collaboration

**Tool:** `python add_product_sync.py`

## 3. Strategic Customer Engagements

**Tool:** `python add_strategic_customer.py`

| What to track | Why it matters |
|---------------|----------------|

| Engagement type | Executive meeting, complex deal, business-outcome focus. |
| Who you met (roles) | Trusted advisor to all levels. |

**Practice Lead bar:** Owns client communication; trusted advisor; leads discussions with/without extensive prep; identifies future problems and opportunities.

## 7. Deals & Business Impact

**Tool:** `python add_deal.py`

| What to track | Why it matters |
|---------------|----------------|
| Deal name, customer | Which opportunities. |
| Your role | Lead, support, technical lead, solution owner. |
| Pipeline / revenue | Quantified business impact. |
| Outcome | Won, in progress, etc. |
| Evidence & impact | Reputation for influencing and closing big deals; scope of responsibility. |

**Practice Lead bar:** SME for deal type; reputation in BU for influencing and closing big deals; leads complex, multi-solution deals.

## Competency categories (use in every activity)

When prompted for **category**, pick the best fit:

- **SCOPE OF RESPONSIBILITY** – SME, reputation, big deals.  
- **INFLUENCE** – Trusted advisor, client communication.  
- **STRATEGIC DIRECTION** – Leading meetings, PM ownership, broad network.  
- **FUNCTIONAL KNOWLEDGE** – Complex deals, discovery, coaching, escalation.  
- **CUSTOMER ENGAGEMENT** – Owning communication, leading discussions, probing.  
- **PROJECT MANAGEMENT SKILLS** – Supporting 1–2 on PM’ing their deals.  
- **TEAM CONTRIBUTION** – Leading, mentoring, Summit/blog/webinar.  
- **PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT** – Enablement, dedicated mentor, presentation mentoring.

---

## Tips

- **Log weekly** – Use calendar/notes so you don’t forget.  
- **Be specific** – Evidence and impact fields are for your promotion narrative.  
- **Show scale** – Adoption counts, audience size, pipeline/revenue where appropriate.  
- **Sustained relationships** – Multiple sessions with the same person are highlighted in the dashboard.  
- **Balance** – Aim for evidence across all eight competencies over time.

For how each competency is defined and how to assess gaps, see **PRACTICE_LEAD_PROMOTION_FRAMEWORK.md**.
