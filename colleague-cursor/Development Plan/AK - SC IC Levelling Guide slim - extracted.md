## Sheet 1: Practice Lead → Managing Principal

| Dimension | Practice Lead | Principal | Sr. Principal | Managing Principal |
|-----------|---------------|-----------|---------------|--------------------|
| **SCOPE OF RESPONSIBILITY** | Acts as SME, go-to for a particular problem/challenge/deal type. Holds a reputation in the BU for influencing and closing big deals. | Go-to person in this area across Adobe and broader ecosystem (partners, customers). Body of work showing role proficiency, thought leadership, innovation, business impact, thinking outside the box. Consistent success across multiple sales cycles, engagements and/or organizations. | Recognized leader and cross-vertical resource in all SC Owned and non-owned Products and processes (e.g. other Adobe Clouds, BUs). Consistent success across multiple sales cycles, engagements and/or organizations. | *(empty in source)* |
| **INFLUENCE** | Owns day-to-day client communication; trusted advisor to all levels of the customer. | Leadership in client industry, organization, KPIs, challenges; established expertise in specific industry. Can diagram/explain process elements and issues in relation to org issues and trends in sufficient detail in discussions and presentations. | Same as Principal. | *(empty)* |
| **STRATEGIC DIRECTION** | Established credibility in leading meetings, driving conversations to push deals forward across a broad network (internal and external); takes responsibility for PM related to own focus area. | Fosters new ideas and concepts for future business and tech needs. Understands how non-Adobe tech and/or competition may affect opportunities. | Same as Principal. | *(empty)* |
| **FUNCTIONAL KNOWLEDGE** | Leads complex deals, multi-solution opportunities with atypical requirements; detailed analysis of use cases and data requirement scoping and discovery; in-depth evaluation of scope and approach; coaches others and escalates documentation to leadership. | Creates new applications for and/or leads development of enablement, tools and resource materials. Leads initiatives to shape future process and organizational strategy with Sr. Leadership. | Same as Principal. | *(empty)* |
| **CUSTOMER ENGAGEMENT** | Owns day-to-day client communication; trusted advisor to all levels. Leads discussions with/without extensive prep. Asks questions ad-hoc based on conversation outcomes. Asks questions to identify future customer problems and opportunities. Understands how/when to probe or challenge. | Leads discussions with/without extensive prep. Ad-hoc questions from conversation outcomes. Questions to identify future customer problems and opportunities. | Same as Principal. | *(empty)* |
| **PROJECT MANAGEMENT SKILLS** | Supports 1–2 team members in their ability to effectively project manage their deals. | Leads multiple, simultaneous complex client opportunities with little to no support. Established credibility in leading, mentoring and managing projects and team to shape peers and drive organizational success. | Same as Principal. | *(empty)* |
| **TEAM CONTRIBUTION** | Established credibility in leading, mentoring and managing projects and team; shapes peers for organizational success. Regularly speaks at Summit, writes quarterly blog posts or own webinars to promote product area or team work. | Works with abstract ideas and situations to solve strategic issues for own product. Partners with BU and Engineering to establish optimal solutions and evangelize to peer group. Proactively partners with broader ecosystem early so resources are ready and approvals/resourcing issues removed. Empowers team to take risks and initiate resolutions or seize opportunities aligned with org goals. Drives team culture to learn from challenges and stay positive despite setbacks or ambiguity. | Creates inclusive, high-performing team environment; open discussion of issues and ideas. Develops and drives best-practice methodology adopted by entire organization. Identifies gaps in support ecosystem and works with leadership to plan. Empowers team; drives culture (same as Principal). | *(empty)* |
| **PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT** | Leads enablement sessions to grow competency. Dedicated mentor to 1–2 team members. Mentors 1–2 team members to grow presentation skills. | Actively mentors multiple team members. Dedicated mentor to multiple SCs throughout the SC org, regardless of team. Frequent stage presenter, often requested by name (Marketing events, Vision Demo Challenge, Summit). | Actively mentors multiple team members. Dedicated mentor to multiple SCs across the org. Builds formal networks with key decision-makers; external spokesperson for Adobe. | *(empty)* |

## Sheet 2: Level 3 – Solutions Consultant & Sr. Solutions Consultant

| Dimension | Solutions Consultant | Sr. Solutions Consultant |
|-----------|----------------------|---------------------------|
| **SCOPE OF RESPONSIBILITY** | Takes responsibility to work independently on assigned tasks and customer engagements; ensures quality work and collateral delivered on time. | Takes responsibility to also help others with their tasks and customer engagements; ensures quality work and collateral delivered on time. |
| **INFLUENCE** | Manages day-to-day with client levels VP & above; has established trusting relationships. | Manages day-to-day client communication, levels VP & above; has established trusting relationships. |
| **STRATEGIC DIRECTION** | Proficiency in all SC Owned Products; expert in a specific area. Understands how owned products work together and impact business; established industry/functional expertise. Synthesizes sound insights and prioritized recommendations; recommendations are complex, done independently. | Mastery in all SC Owned Products; expert in multiple areas. Same business/industry understanding. Synthesizes sound insights and prioritized recommendations; complex with no guidance and supports team members to do the same. |
| **FUNCTIONAL KNOWLEDGE** | Successfully demos/presents product area in a technically accurate manner; builds custom demo/presentation without oversight; handles troubleshooting independently. Creates high-quality deliverables against sales methodology (POVs, EAA, Tech Case, POC Plan) independently. Actively contributes to account team strategy to drive CJ forward. Communicates with product management for enhancements. | Same demo/present and custom build; troubleshooting independently. Communicates with product management for enhancements. Effectively creates high-quality, highly creative deliverables (POVs, EAA, Tech Case, POC Plan). Actively leads and steers account team strategy to drive CJ forward. Flags deal risk in timely manner; surfaces risks and brainstorms solutions or paths forward. |
| **CUSTOMER ENGAGEMENT** | Comfort with client industry, organization, KPIs, challenges; established expertise in specific industry. Leads ongoing dialog that creates excitement and momentum in the selling cycle. Comfortable with atypical requirements and unexpected scenarios; detailed analysis of use cases and in-depth evaluation of scope and approach. | Same industry/org comfort. Can uncover future-state issues on customers’ most critical issues during discovery; empathizes and relates from experience with similar challenges. Comfortable with atypical, unprecedented asks; detailed analysis and in-depth evaluation of scope and approach. |
| **PROJECT MANAGEMENT SKILLS** | Leads complex client opportunities with minimal support. Identifies and procures appropriate resources. Defines and project manages internal/external stakeholders with minimal support. | Leads complex client opportunities with no support. Procures appropriate resources at the right time by planning and communicating. Defines and project manages all internal/external stakeholders with no support. Proactively anticipates risks and escalates to leadership. Regularly demonstrates good judgment in delivery execution and supports ongoing conversations for positive results. |
| **TEAM CONTRIBUTION** | Active role in a project that helps grow and scale the SC Organization. Manages internal initiatives (e.g. enablement workstream, hiring workstream). | Identifies and leads project that helps grow and scale the SC Organization. Shares opinions and communicates goals with confidence to inform decisions. Owner of internal initiatives (e.g. enablement workstream, participates in hiring panels). Identifies ways to improve outdated/incorrect processes or documentation; takes initiative to share with team and cross-functional BU teams. |
| **PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT** | Mentors and helps guide career of more junior SCs and team members. Confident presenter; can read and manage a room and adjust in real time. Delivers compelling presentations with positive account team and/or client feedback. | Mentors and helps guide career of more junior SCs and team members. Acts as ambassador for direct team in the ecosystem. Exceptional presenter; consistently generates emotional response and positive feedback from audience. |
