## Summary: Practice Lead in one view

- **SME** for a problem/challenge/deal type; known in the BU for influencing and closing big deals.
- **Trusted advisor** to the customer at all levels; owns day-to-day client communication.
- **Leads** meetings and conversations across a broad internal and external network; owns PM for own focus area.
- **Leads** complex, multi-solution deals with atypical requirements; runs discovery, scoping, and analysis; coaches others and escalates to leadership.
- **Leads** discussions with or without heavy prep; asks ad-hoc and forward-looking questions; probes and challenges appropriately.
- **Supports 1–2** people to project manage their deals.
- **Leads, mentors, and manages** projects and team; shapes peers for org success.
- **External voice**: Summit sessions, quarterly blog posts or webinars for product/team.
- **Enablement**: Leads sessions to grow competency; **dedicated mentor to 1–2**; mentors 1–2 on presentation skills.

---

## Next level: Sr. Practice Lead (for reference)

- Same SME and trusted-advisor bar, plus **C-level** communication and knowledge transfer to peers, partners, and customers.
- **Leads initiatives** to shape future process and organizational strategy.
- **Strategic accounts**: uncovers needs and pain points; works with abstract ideas to solve strategic issues; partners with BU and Engineering; evangelizes to peer group.
- **Discovery**: uncovers future-state issues on customers’ most critical issues; empathizes and relates from experience.
- **Supports 3+** team members on project management.
- **External spokesperson**: builds formal networks with key decision makers.
- **Dedicated mentor to 3+**; mentors 3+ on presentation skills.
