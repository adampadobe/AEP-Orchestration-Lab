Preventing Cancer

Our research led to the development of the HPV vaccine which is expected to prevent almost 90% of cervical cancer cases in the UK1.

Detecting Cancer

In the last 50 years, our work has helped double cancer survival in the UK. Finding new ways to detect cancer at an early stage has played an important role in this.

Treating Cancer

More than 8 in 10 people who receive cancer drugs in the UK receive a drug developed by us, or with us1. We are pioneering new treatments.
