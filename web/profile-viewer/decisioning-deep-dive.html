Industry

Media context. Teams align on subscription SKUs, trial windows, and content metadata in the schema; collections often segment by genre or tier; rules gate promos by engagement and tier.
Travel context. Schema carries route, cabin, and cut-off fields; collections slice ancillaries by haul and fare; rules combine loyalty tier and trip timing.
Retail context. Schema ties to SKU, margin, and promo type; collections mirror merchandising folders; rules use cart, browse, and loyalty signals.
FSI context. Schema reflects regulated product lines and disclosures; collections separate lending, cards, and wealth; rules enforce consent and channel.
Telco context. Schema covers plans, speed tiers, and add-ons; collections split mobile, fixed, and SMB pools; rules use serviceability and journey stage.
Automotive context. Schema encodes programme, stock, and region; collections separate retail, EV, and aftersales; rules use buyer type and service timing.
Healthcare context. Schema maps pathways and benefits; collections group virtual, specialty, and employer programmes; rules respect coverage and clinical eligibility.
