🎨

Asset extraction

Image URLs, favicons, Open Graph previews, colour palette, and font families — surfaced per scrape. Click Classify images on any result to download up to 20 images to Cloud Storage and categorise with Gemini vision.

✍

Brand guidelines

AI-drafted tone of voice, brand values, editorial rules, channel messaging guides. Runs automatically on Analyse.

🚀

Campaign detection

Detects 2-5 campaigns visible on the site (with source URL evidence) plus 3-4 AI-recommended demo campaigns — each with headlines, CTA, channel, and target segments.

👤

Customer personas

6 realistic personas with demographics, goals, pain points, behaviours, channel preferences, and suggested segments — generated for your chosen Persona country.

🎯

Audience segments

8-10 Real-Time CDP segments across edge / streaming / batch evaluation — each with criteria, estimated size, suggested campaigns, and qualified personas. Grounded in the generated personas and campaigns.

🧑‍💼

Business stakeholders

Extracts named leadership, founders, and key people from About / Leadership / Team pages. Builds an org chart grouped by seniority (C-suite, VP, Director, etc.) with headshots pulled from the site's image catalogue.

🏷️

Tag & analytics audit

Per-page inventory of Adobe Launch / Web SDK, Google Tag Manager / GA4, Tealium, Segment, CMPs, and other common vendors — plus duplicate collector hints, slow third-party scripts, and console errors when JS-rendered crawl is on. Toggle it under Options; outputs appear in results after Analyse.

📦

Export kit

Click Export kit (ZIP) on any result to download a ZIP containing brand guidelines (MD + JSON), personas, campaigns, segments, raw crawl, colour palette, and all classified images. Signed URL expires in 72h; bucket auto-deletes at 3 days.
